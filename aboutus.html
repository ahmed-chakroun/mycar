About Us – My Car

Driven by Innovation. Powered by Passion.

Founded in 2011, My Car was born from a single idea: that the future of mobility should be bold, intelligent, and exhilarating. Based in NeoCity, California, My Car is a next-generation automotive company designing and manufacturing high-performance electric and hybrid vehicles with a sharp focus on sustainability, smart technology, and driver experience.

From our first prototype—the Vortex Pulse—to our flagship electric SUV, the Vortex Stride, we’ve consistently pushed boundaries in design, efficiency, and innovation. Our vehicles are not only built to move people—they’re built to inspire.

Our Mission

To revolutionize transportation by creating vehicles that are environmentally responsible, beautifully designed, and technologically ahead of their time.

Our Vision

A world where driving is cleaner, smarter, and more thrilling than ever before.

Core Values

  • Innovation – We never stop exploring what’s next.
  • Sustainability – Our commitment to the planet drives every decision.
  • Integrity – Transparency, quality, and customer trust are our foundation.
  • Experience – We design with the driver at heart.

Our Global Impact

With R&D centers in California, Germany, and South Korea, and delivery networks spanning over 25 countries, My Car is rapidly becoming a global force in clean mobility.
